# 2022-1 Linear Algebra

- Week 8:(4/21): `Midterm`

- Week 13 (5/26): Least squares & Normal Equation & Gram-Schmidt
- Week 14 (6/2): QR decomposition & Eigenvalue decomposition
- Week 15 (6/9): Singular value decomposition & PCA
- Week 16 (6/16): `Final exam`
